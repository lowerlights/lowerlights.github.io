About Lower Lights Goals

Lower Lights Goals is created for the sole purpose for you to create goals. We focus on letting you be able to keep track of goals, create them, and make sure you know they need to be done. You are a great person, you can do great things. And with Lower Lights goals, you can achieve great things much faster.

Lower Lights Goals was created by John Kimball. The website and software itself was created by TomMustBe12.

If you want to keep yourself up to date and get more stuff done, this is the place to do it.
